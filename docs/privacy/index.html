Sky Hopper Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Sky Hopper does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app contains no accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs.

The highest score is stored only on the device using Apple's UserDefaults and is not transmitted anywhere. Removing the app also removes this local data.

For support or privacy questions, open an issue in the Sky Hopper repository.
