# Sky Hopper Asset Provenance

All raster assets were generated specifically for Sky Hopper with OpenAI's built-in image generation tool on 2026-07-10. No third-party image, logo, character, or product reference was supplied.

## Shared direction

- Style: crisp 2D pixel art, original arcade-game identity
- Palette: deep navy and violet sunset, turquoise bird, coral floating towers, gold star
- Constraints: no text, logos, trademarks, watermarks, or recognizable third-party characters

## Final prompt set

### `bird.png` — 68×48 RGBA

> One original right-facing turquoise mechanical sky bird with a compact horizontal silhouette, raised angular wing, gold beak, deep-navy outline and coral accent. Sparse authentic 16-bit pixel art using large solid clusters and at most six flat colors. Exactly one eye, wing, body, short tail and beak; no legs, gears, bolts or micro-detail. Center on a perfectly flat `#00ff00` chroma-key background with generous padding. No text, logo, trademark, watermark, gradients, antialiasing or resemblance to an existing game character.

### `background.png` — 288×512 RGB

> Original portrait 9:16 endless-sky sunset fading from deep navy through violet and magenta to coral-orange, with sparse blocky clouds and tiny distant floating islands along the lower horizon. Authentic 16-bit pixel environment with a calm central play area. Full-bleed and opaque; no foreground obstacles, bird, towers, stars, UI, text, logo, trademark or watermark.

### `pipe.png` — 52×320 RGBA

> One original narrow coral-magenta floating-sky tower, front-facing and perfectly vertical, with a chunky top cap and a simple repeatable shaft, turquoise energy seams and deep-navy outline. Sparse 16-bit pixel sprite on a perfectly flat `#00ff00` chroma-key background. No bottom cap, plumbing shape, bird, star, ground, text, logo, trademark or watermark.

### `ground.png` — 336×112 RGB

> Seamless horizontal dark-violet floating-island ground strip with a level coral-magenta top rim and sparse turquoise energy cracks. Wide 3:1 opaque 16-bit pixel texture with tileable left and right edges. No sky, bird, tower, star, text, logo, trademark, watermark, green grass or perspective angle.

### `star.png` — 40×40 RGBA

> One centered symmetrical gold five-point energy star with orange inner shading, thick deep-navy pixel outline and two small turquoise spark pixels. Sparse 16-bit pixel sprite with a strong small-size silhouette on a perfectly flat `#00ff00` chroma-key background. No glow blur, text, logo, trademark or watermark.

### `AppIcon.png` — 1024×1024 RGB

> Opaque square iOS icon showing the same simplified turquoise sky bird hopping upward toward one gold star across a navy-violet-coral pixel sunset. Large deliberate 16-bit pixel clusters, bird lower-left, star upper-right, simple cloud and floating-island silhouettes, generous safe margin. Exactly one bird and star; no text, border, rounded-corner mask, logo, trademark, watermark or recognizable third-party character.
